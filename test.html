Robot Meena YouTube Video

Three students of the Electronics Department of Lakshipur Polytechnic Institute, Shantanu Acharjee, Mehraj Hossain Sagar, and Yasin Arafat Riday, have made a robot named Meena to entertain children as well as to study. Robot Meena has attracted the most attention on social media. The theme of the 43rd Science Fair at Lakshmipur was "Mobile Phone Addiction Harms to Studies". Meena the robot has been created keeping in mind the subject of matrimony.

All the features that Robot Mina has

  • Can speak Bengali and English.
  • Can hear and understand the Bengali language.
  • Can answer various questions.
  • Can recite poems and songs.
  • Hands can do mischief.
  • Can turn lights and fans on and off.
  • Can make phone calls.
  • Can take pictures.
  • Can play games with you.

Shantanu and his friends first tried to find out why children are getting addicted to phones. Then they realize that children have a lack of playground and lack of friends due to which TV and mobile phones are becoming more dominant among children. Robot Meena will help children study with entertainment. They will answer various questions. Meena Robot can recite children's poems, songs and stories. As a result, the robot will spend some time away from the child's mobile phone and spend time with Meena. Meena is made as a friend.

Apart from education and entertainment, Meena can be used for home security. Meena will sound the alarm as well as make a phone call to your phone when there is a fire in the home. As a result, even without being present at the house, you will understand that there is a fire in the house. As a result, you can take the necessary measures. Meena will be further developed in the future, work is in progress so that Meena can move on her own. Moreover, Meena is being taught various educational games. Every elementary school needs a robot, Meena. So that children can learn about robots from an early age.

Robot Meena uses a microphone to listen to what we say to Meena when someone calls her Meena Bowl in front of Meena. The data of what we are saying through the microphone is going to the Raspberry Pi computer in Meena. The Raspberry processes what we say and answers the questions stored in its memory to us through the speaker. And this entire process is done using Python programming language. C++ programming language is used to control the motor and hand movement. Work is going on to improve Meena in the future.

All the components needed to build the Robot Meena

  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduino Uno Mega
  • Servo Motor
  • Mini Sound Box
  • BOYA BY-M1 Microphone
  • Relay Module
  • Infrared Module
  • Led
  • 10k ohm Resistor
  • Python C++ Language
